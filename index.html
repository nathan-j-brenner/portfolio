About

Alan Moore said “All we ever see of stars are their old photographs.” I grew up in a bright town where I rarely saw the stars, so the times I have are fond memories. I've worn a lot of hats in the last decade. I've been a soldier, a musician, a teacher, a husband, a father. Each role I played stands out like a single star. When I look out at the night sky, I don't see one individual star, just like I don't consider myself defined by any single attribute.

I have a core set of values: Integrity, honesty, accountability, creativity, and collaboration.

My first teaching job was in a tiny town, and the staff used Google docs. It took me most of the year to figure out that technology. Before that time, I used computers mostly for writing papers and I was proud that I could typically take any new software and figure out how to use it. A few months ago I chose to leave public education. My primary focus right now is technology. Check out my web prense on some of the sites on this page and decide for yourself if my current skillset can meet your needs.
